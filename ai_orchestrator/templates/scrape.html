How Web Scraping Works
What We Scrape:
  • Restaurant and venue information from Yelp
  • Date-related events from Eventbrite
  • Tourist attractions from TripAdvisor
  • Ratings, reviews, and pricing information
  • Location and category data
Ethical Scraping:
  • Respectful delays between requests
  • Only public information is collected
  • Automatic deduplication of results
  • Rate-limited to avoid server overload
  • No personal or private data collection
After Scraping:

Scraped ideas are automatically processed to generate vector embeddings for semantic search. Duplicate entries are removed, and all ideas are tagged with their source for transparency.
